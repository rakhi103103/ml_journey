# 🛒 Customer Behavior and Sales Intelligence

## What This Project Does
Analysis of customer purchase behavior and sales patterns
to identify revenue trends and top customers.

## Skills Used
- **Pandas**: groupby, pivot_table, crosstab, transform
- **NumPy**: np.select, np.where, percentile analysis
- **Matplotlib**: bar charts

## Key Insights
- Identified top customers by total revenue
- Categorized orders into Small, Medium and Large segments
- Analyzed revenue by region and product category
